Introduction

Sophos Advisory Services and Sophos Professional Services from Sophos help organizations prepare for, respond to, and recover from cybersecurity incidents. The official Sophos services catalog describes standardized advisory and professional offerings that are delivered using Service Units, with Sophos-defined scopes and stated outcomes. Published Service Unit (SU) counts depend on your organization's size and engagement objectives; complex scope may require additional SUs estimated before commencement. Services are ordinarily delivered in English; ask your Sophos representative if you need another language.

This questionnaire helps you explore the catalog without deep prior knowledge of every service. Select what interests you, add scoping notes, and export a PDF summary for discussion with your Sophos account team or partner. It does not replace the catalog, sizing, or your statement of work, and it does not submit responses to Sophos.

How to Use This Questionnaire

  1. Start with Scoping Questions to get personalized service recommendations
  2. Review the recommended services and adjust any selections as needed
  3. Answer the per-service scoping questions to describe your environment and priorities
  4. Fill in your contact information
  5. Download the PDF summary for your Sophos account team, partner, or internal stakeholders
